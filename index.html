About Us ST.PETER’S CENTRAL PUBLIC SCHOOL WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1989-90 UNDER THE AEGIS OF ROMAN CATHOLICS AND ST PETER’S EDUCATIONAL ASSOCIATION-HANAMKONDA. IT IS LOCATED IN THE HEART OF TRI CITIES AT ST PETER’S COLONY, OLD BUS DEPOT ROAD, HANAMKONDA. THOUSANDS OF STUDENTS PASSED OF OUR INSTITUTION AND EXPLORED THROUGH OUT THE WORLD. WITH THE ENCOURAGEMENT OF STUDENTS, FACULTY, PARENTS AND ALUMNI WE HAVE INTRODUCED ST PETER’S SENIOR SECONDARY SCHOOL FOR CLASS XI & XII.
